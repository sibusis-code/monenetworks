# Mone Networks - Professional Website Landing Page

## 🎨 Design Overview

A modern, conversion-focused landing page for Mone Networks, a South African web design agency offering professional websites with R0 upfront costs.

**Primary Colors (Trust & Professional):**
- **Primary Blue**: #0066CC (Trust, stability, professionalism)
- **Accent Green**: #00A86B (Growth, success, prosperity)
- **Navy**: #1A2332 (Authority, sophistication)

**Supporting Colors:**
- **Teal Accent**: #0E9AA7 (Innovation, freshness)
- **Gray Scale**: Various shades for text and backgrounds
- **White**: #FFFFFF (Clean, modern)

### Typography
- **Font Family**: Inter (Professional, modern, highly readable)
- **Headings**: Bold weights (700-800) for impact
- **Body Text**: Regular weight (400-500) for readability

## 🎨 Logo Assets

The Mone Networks logo is inspired by your brand identity, featuring:
- **MN Monogram**: Clean, professional serif-style letterforms
- **Company Name**: Bold sans-serif "MONE NETWORKS" text
- **Accent Line**: Gradient accent using brand colors (blue to green)

**Available Formats**:
- `logo.svg` - Main logo for website use
- `favicon.svg` - Browser tab icon (shows in browser tabs)
- `apple-touch-icon.svg` - iOS/Apple device icon
- All icons use your brand gradient (Blue #0066CC to Green #00A86B)

## ️ To Customize

1. **Logo**: Replace `.logo-text` with your actual logo image
2. **Images**: Add relevant images to service cards and hero section
3. **Form Action**: Connect the contact form to your email/CRM system
4. **Colors**: Adjust CSS variables in `:root` for brand customization
5. **Content**: Update copy to match your specific offerings

## 📧 Form Integration

The contact form currently logs to console. To integrate with your backend:

1. Add form action URL
2. Connect to email service (e.g., EmailJS, FormSpree)
3. Or integrate with your CRM (e.g., HubSpot, Salesforce)
